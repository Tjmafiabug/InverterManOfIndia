These weren’t your average corporate workshops; they were bold, experiential journeys designed to push our boundaries. At the heart of this culture was Mr. Kunwer Sachdev, Founder of Su-Kam—a man whose dynamic leadership and infectious energy turned every challenge into an opportunity for growth.</p><p>Kunwer Sachdev wasn’t just the visionary behind Su-Kam’s technology and business innovation—he was the soul of the organization. A natural motivator and an exceptional trainer, he believed that real learning happens when people step outside their comfort zones. Whether it was a sales strategy session or a team-building activity, he made sure people weren’t just attending—they were evolving.</p><p>In one such training camp, an experience etched permanently in my memory took place. People hesitated. The thought of walking over red-hot embers seemed insane. And that’s when Kunwer sir, as always, did what only he could do—he stepped forward without hesitation and walked across the fire.</p><p>He got slightly burned that first time—his feet had blisters. But instead of stopping, he ran across it again, with even more conviction. He knew that what people needed in that moment wasn’t just instruction—they needed an example. His fearlessness lit a fire in all of us—not on the coal bed, but in our minds and hearts.</p><figure id="figure-744mclowgjmcbmlmdt"><img id="744mclowgjmcbmlmdt" src="/assets/24bdtasZ6MtIqFbvWSQBii9GmrZeDG0r6dWecENYXnrswQNwqu3NFgBcFh6w7BEB.jpg" alt="" title="" class="object-cover bg-gray-100 rounded-2xl md:rounded-3xl lg:rounded-4xl" style="aspect-ratio: 16/9; object-position: center center;" data-corners="default" data-aspect="16:9" data-caption="" data-positionX="undefined" data-positionY="undefined" data-fullWidth="false"/></figure><p>His charisma was unmatched—not the kind that came from a title, but the kind that came from courage, presence, and authenticity. Under his leadership, fear gave way to confidence. One after another, we all walked across the fire. Around 40 of us, including many of the women participants, conquered that moment. What started as fear turned into celebration. People were cheering, high-fiving, hugging—it was a breakthrough we all shared.</p><p>That day wasn’t about physical endurance. It was about overcoming the limits we set for ourselves. And none of it would’ve happened if Kunwer Sachdev hadn’t shown us how it’s done—not just by leading from the front, but by leading with heart.</p><p>This wasn’t an isolated incident either. It was a reflection of who he was—a bold experimenter, a constant learner, and a leader who truly believed in the potential of his people. Whether it was building cutting-edge technology, redefining the power backup industry, or turning corporate training into life-changing experiences, Kunwer Sachdev always had a unique way of doing things—unconventional, impactful, and unforgettable.</p><p><br></p><p><br></p>
