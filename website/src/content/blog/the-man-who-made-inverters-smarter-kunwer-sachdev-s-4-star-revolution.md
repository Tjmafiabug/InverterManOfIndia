His design didn’t just save energy — it redefined what efficiency looked like. While traditional systems bled power silently, his high-frequency inverter ensured that every watt counted.</p><p>This wasn’t just engineering.<br>It was empathy in design.</p><h3>&nbsp;<br>A Star That Lit the Path for Others</h3><p>Kunwer didn’t just stop at building a product.<br>He built India’s first in-house R&amp;D unit for inverter technology, recognized by the Government of India back in 2002. That R&amp;D unit became the birthplace of innovation — where ideas turned into patents, and prototypes turned into real solutions for millions.</p><p>His high-frequency inverter wasn’t just a UPS. It was a silent guardian of productivity, making sure homes and offices stayed lit, connected, and alive — even when the grid failed.</p><h3>&nbsp;<br>A Legacy of Firsts</h3><p>Even today, while the market is crowded with brands, it was Kunwer Sachdev who wrote the rulebook.</p><p>From pioneering Battery Equalization to leading in energy star ratings, from dreaming of smart solar solutions to making them a reality in 70+ countries — he has always stayed years ahead of his time.</p><p>It’s no wonder that his journey is not just about a company or a product.<br>It’s about a man who quietly, consistently, and passionately shaped an entire industry — so the rest could follow.</p><p>Disclaimer</p><p>Mr. Kunwer Sachdev, the original founder and visionary behind Su-Kam, is no longer associated with Su-Kam Power Systems Ltd.
